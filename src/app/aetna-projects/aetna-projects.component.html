Aetna - Google Search Map Integration

Collaborating with UX research to ensure best practices, I developed a Google Search API integrated prototype, enhancing healthcare access.

MY ROLE

In my capacity as the UX Lead at Aetna Behavioral Health, I managed the creation of a map-centric web prototype, employing best practices to advance the user experience.

PROCESS

Our main aim was to make it easier for our members, particularly in the behavioral health area, to find top healthcare providers efficiently, reflecting our commitment to cost-effective solutions for our customers.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

Using insights from user research and pilot testing, we designed a prototype that combines the Google Search API's functionality with custom pin icons and a map for easy navigation. This work demonstrates our focus on the unique needs of behavioral health users and the effectiveness of our team collaboration.
