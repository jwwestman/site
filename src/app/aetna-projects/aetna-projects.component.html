Aetna - Rewards Merchandise Strategy

An initiative designed to seamlessly weave fitness merchandise into the existing rewards ecosystem, setting the stage for future expansions and enhancing the user shopping journey.

MY ROLE

As the UX Lead, I was entrusted with the challenge of seamlessly integrating fitness merchandise into Aetna's rewards platform.

PROCESS

My journey began with fostering collaborative vision-crafting sessions on the Miro Board, which paved the way for a redefined rewards homepage that resonated across mobile, tablet, and desktop interfaces.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

A key accomplishment was the revamp of the user journey, introducing a novel entry point that made the checkout process smoother and inherently intuitive. But this wasn't just about the immediate; I also laid the foundation for a broader merchandise assortment slated for 2023, always keeping an ear to the ground for user feedback and emerging market trends.

This project's breadth went beyond mere aesthetics, delving into both front-end user interactions and backend functionalities to ensure a harmonized and enriching shopping experience in the rewards arena.
