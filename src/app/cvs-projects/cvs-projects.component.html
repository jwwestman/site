CVS Health - Kiosk PreCheck-in Experience

A transformative approach to enhancing the patient precheck-in journey.

MY ROLE

As the Design Lead, I spearheaded the integration of traditional kiosk usage into a full-fledged web experience.

PROCESS

By creating adaptive workflows, we catered to all users, including those without mobile phones. I introduced QR code functionality for a streamlined pre-check, ensuring the user experience was consistent on 10-inch Android tablets.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

Through strategic collaborations, we seamlessly blended classic check-in practices with our new web-centric approach.

CVS Health - Journey Map Workshop Template

A pioneering initiative focused on elevating care management through a meticulously crafted tool capturing real-world patient experiences.

MY ROLE

As the UX Lead, I steered the synthesis of insights from a range of stakeholders, including Commercial members and CVS care teams.

PROCESS

This comprehensive approach catered to the unique needs of all, from regular members to those transitioning to prospective Medicare plans.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

With these insights, I designed a versatile Journey Map template. More than just a discussion starter in workshops, this template set a foundational tone, fostering the development of high fidelity journey maps pivotal for leadership insights.

Ultimately, this wasn’t just a standalone project—it became a cornerstone, shaping strategies across the entire Health Portfolio.
