Fidelity - My Prime Progressive Web App

Creating a platform for hedge fund managers, optimizing securities lending and risk management.

MY ROLE

For My Prime, another significant project, I immersed myself in the user environment by joining traders on the capital markets floor. This hands-on research was pivotal in developing an MVP that stands as a benchmark for next-generation progressive web applications.

PROCESS

In our initial research and discovery, we delved into the traders' pain points with the help of third-party analysts and consultants, gaining a nuanced understanding. This knowledge, coupled with new technologies, has been instrumental in enhancing performance and refining the user interface to address their needs effectively with each successive update.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

Our deliverables included a functional prototype, informed by insights into traders' challenges and bolstered by new technology. This prototype has become a crucial tool for testing and refining the user experience, leading to tangible improvements in their daily performance.
