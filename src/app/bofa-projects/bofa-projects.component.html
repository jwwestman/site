BANK OF AMERICA

At Bank of America, I championed pivotal projects that marked a turning point in digital banking experiences—from revolutionizing the internal hiring system with a next-gen workforce portal, to innovating customer deals with responsive design and gamification, and enhancing the Advantage Banking service to seamlessly cater to a spectrum of banking needs across all platforms. Each project was a step towards setting new industry standards for technology and user engagement.

Bank of America - Next-Gen Workforce Portal

This forward-thinking initiative was aimed at revolutionizing Bank of America's internal hiring landscape. It was designed to meet the unique needs of the authenticated audience, setting new benchmarks in recruitment processes.

MY ROLE

As the UX Lead, my focus was steadfastly user-centric. I spearheaded the creation of intuitive digital pathways for diverse bank roles and significantly enhanced the super admin experience to ensure a fluid and intuitive navigation journey.

PROCESS

The process was deeply collaborative, involving the crafting and finetuning of digital solutions that catered to the specific workflows within the bank.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

I led the development and testing of innovative prototypes, engaging with super-admins, hiring managers, and recruiters through moderated user testing. The insights garnered were pivotal in refining our strategies and directly influenced the successful launch of a streamlined hiring platform.

This platform not only struck a chord with our internal teams but also brought new efficiencies to our recruitment workflow.

Bank of America - BankAmeriDeals

BankAmeriDeals stands as a testament to Bank of America's commitment to incorporating responsive design with gamification, setting the stage for next-gen digital initiatives.

MY ROLE

In my role as Senior Interaction Designer, I championed the BankAmeriDeals project, pushing the boundaries of traditional banking experiences to innovate and ensure adaptability across user interfaces.

PROCESS

Our process was immersive and collaborative. We designed prototypes with the future in mind, testing them with real users from the BankAmeriDeal gaming community. These moderated sessions provided us with critical insights, shaping a user experience that exemplified best practices in the field.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

The prototypes we crafted, though not currently in production, represent best practices and serve as blueprints for future digital breakthroughs.

The feedback from our extensive user testing has been distilled into a strategic arsenal, ready to be deployed in upcoming projects that reflect our vision for BankAmeriDeals and beyond.

Bank of America - Bank of America Advantage Banking

Advantage Banking exemplifies Bank of America's dedication to seamless integration and customer enrichment, marrying streamlined interfaces with a rewards system that grants financial advantages to clients sustaining a $10,000 balance. This initiative has markedly advanced customer contentment and operational prowess.

MY ROLE

As the UX Lead for this transformative initiative, I orchestrated a harmony of strategy, collaboration, and execution.

PROCESS

Working alongside teams from content strategy to engineering, we reimagined everyday banking.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

Our introductions of SafeBalance Banking® – a forward-thinking checkless system, Advantage Plus – which simplifies fee waivers, and Advantage Relationship – honoring those with higher balances, are testaments to our commitment to user-centric design and innovation.

Together, we've ensured a consistent, intuitive experience across every touchpoint.
