BANK OF AMERICA

At Bank of America, I led transformative projects that reshaped digital banking. I revolutionized the internal hiring system with an advanced workforce portal, introduced responsive design and gamification for customer deals, and upgraded the Advantage Banking service for diverse needs across platforms. Each initiative distinguished itself by pushing industry benchmarks for tech and user interaction.

Bank of America - Next-Gen Workforce Portal

Revamping Bank of America's recruitment process with innovative design solutions.

MY ROLE

I championed the 'Revamping Recruitment' project at Bank of America, where design and experience architecture were key in refining our internal hiring process. My efforts focused on optimizing digital navigation for banking roles and enhancing the super admin experience with a tailored dashboard for efficient tracking and notifications.

PROCESS

The collaborative and research-driven development process was guided by user feedback to ensure practical and user-friendly design solutions.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

Leading the prototype development and user testing, I centered our design on the real-world needs of hiring managers and super admins. The updated platform has since streamlined recruitment processes and received positive internal feedback for its improved efficiency and user experience.

Bank of America - BankAmeriDeals Revamp

Leading the BankAmeriDeals project, I focused on unifying the native app and responsive web experiences to enhance consistency and user engagement in our community.

MY ROLE

As the Senior Interaction Designer and de facto Experience Architect, my role in the BankAmeriDeals project involved harmonizing responsive design with gamification elements, elevating the user interface of our cash back rewards program.

PROCESS

Our method was hands-on and team-oriented, developing prototypes and gleaning insights from real user testing within the BankAmeriDeal community.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

Our work on BankAmeriDeals has led to prototypes that set benchmarks for digital innovation, with key features now in production. The user feedback collected continues to inform strategic enhancements, steadily shaping the evolution of BankAmeriDeals.

Bank of America - Advantage Checking Enhanced

Merging UX redesign with Advantage Checking Enhanced at Bank of America, elevating banking experiences.

MY ROLE

As the UX Lead for this transformative initiative, I orchestrated a harmony of strategy, collaboration, and execution.

PROCESS

Working alongside teams from content strategy to engineering, we reimagined everyday banking.

DELIVERABLES & OUTCOMES

Our introductions of SafeBalance Banking® – a forward-thinking checkless system, Advantage Plus – which simplifies fee waivers, and Advantage Relationship – honoring those with higher balances, are testaments to our commitment to user-centric design and innovation.

Together, we've ensured a consistent, intuitive experience across every touchpoint.
